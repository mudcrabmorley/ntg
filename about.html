ABOUT NTG FIGHT & FITNESS

Founded by the world-renowned trainer and fight promoter Nugget McNaught, NTG is a revered brand in Australian fight sports and beyond. Known for producing multiple champions over the decades, our team offers more than 30 years of experience in the fight sports industry!

Now, NTG Fight & Fitness offers members a unique and unparalleled training experience by providing a performance training centre located centrally between Brisbane and the Gold Coast, Queensland.

The NTG Fight & Fitness performance centre boasts a functional and strength-based training facility, Muay Thai/MMA/Boxing facilities, and a range of kids' classes which aim to support members from all walks of life and levels of skill.

From the fitness enthusiast to the world champion fighter, NTG Fight & Fitness welcomes all and strives to provide our members with the support they need to fulfil their health, fitness, and lifestyle goals. Whether it be weight loss, transformation, increasing strength, or improving your skills for competition, NTG Fight & Fitness invites you to become part of the team, where together we can become fitter, stronger, and greater.
